Top PR & Media Relations Agency in India

Deesha Communications is one of the top PR agencies in Mumbai, specialising in strategic public relations (PR), Analyst Relations, Digital PR, Strategic Crisis Management, Celebrity Endorsement Study,  Media Audit and  Branding Research for any level of PR campaign with strong agency presence in  Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Kolkata, and Guwahati.

Our rich experience of strategic media management, marketing intelligence and a strong media relations play very critical role to come up with very strong PR strategies to ensure higher ROI for any level of PR campaign. Our in-house media research plays pivotal role to strengthen media relations for brands and organizations in different industries.

 

As one of the top PR agencies in Mumbai, Deesha Communications offers very strong and result oriented media relations across India for any level of PR campaign. Media relations, which is very important for any successful public relations program in India, is one of the core strengths of Deesha Communications. To create a strong media relations, a consistence and planned media management is very important. Only contacts of journalists can never produce a healthy and positive media relations for any level of PR campaign. As one of the top PR agencies in Mumbai, our media relations initiatives are supported by well designed strategy, in-house media research and strategic content development initiatives.    


 The media relations in India has undergone significant changes over the last 10-to-15 years. Media research and editorial value of the contents are getting more and more importance in developing strong media relations for any successful PR campaign.
